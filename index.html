Mental Health From Social Media

The aim of the project is to detect mental health issues from social media posts of users with a high accuracy. We scrape a dataset of posts from users and train our model on the data based on self-reporting of mental health issues. We use AWD-LSTM model for the same and use the model for determination of mental health issues such as stress and anxiety from users' subjective responses. The project is currently under research and development with Dr. Jyoti Joshi, Research Fellow at Monash University.

Microsoft Kaizala Chatbot

Done as a part of internship project at Microsoft IDC, Hyderabad. The project involved development of a Chatbot channel for Microsoft's mobile chatting application and also a chatbot using Luis.ai with Intent Classification and Slot tagging.

Smart Tweets with Sentiment Analysis

The project aims to help brands and companies design best of the tweets in least amount of time. We take the input of brand name and location from the user and use this input to come up with keywords and retweet suggestions for the next tweet which is expected to be more popular based on sentiment and semantic analysis of previous tweets. Recommendation Systems

The report was writter as a part of the term paper of course Applied AI under Dr. Shashi Shekhar Jha. The report analyses the improvement in traditional collaborative filtering with the use of matrix factorization and Neural Networks. The implementation is done in Keras and is available here.

Explainable Medical Report Generation

Working on the model to have chest x ray reports through an explainable model useful for practical use of deep learning black box models with Dr. Abhinav Dhall, Monash University and Dr. Ramanathan, IIT Ropar. The aim is to have a Cycle-Gan based model which maps reports and chest x ray images. We have attained competitive Natural Language Metrics on our generated reports and the further development is under progress.
